## Challenge

TechPay Solutions, a 200-employee fintech, needed ISO 27001 certification to meet regulatory requirements for the European market. Their security team was lean (3 people) with no prior certification experience.

Key challenges included:
- No formal documentation of security processes
- Multiple disconnected tools for risk management
- Tight deadline: certification needed within 8 months

## Solution

Using Riskitera, TechPay adopted a unified approach:

1. **Automated risk assessment** using the Risk Analytics module
2. **Control mapping** against ISO 27001 Annex A with the GRC module
3. **Automatic evidence generation** through the Evidence Vault
4. **Continuous monitoring** with the integrated SOC module

## Results

- **6 months** to certification (vs. 12-18 month industry average)
- **70% less time** spent on evidence collection
- **100% of controls** mapped and documented
- **Zero non-conformities** in the certification audit
